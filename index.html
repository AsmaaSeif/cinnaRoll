About CinnaRoll

CinaRoll: Our Homemade Promise

At CinaRoll, we don't just bake; we craft happiness, right from our home to yours. There's a secret ingredient in every roll we make, and that's pure, heartfelt passion.

Forget the factory line—our commitment is to homemade perfection. Every morning, our kitchen is filled with the irresistible aroma of cinnamon. We select the finest ingredients, blend them with love, and let time work its magic to create that melt-in-your-mouth texture.

We believe the best moments are the simplest ones. That’s why every swirl is handmade to bring you a treat that feels like a warm hug.

Experience the difference of a roll made with true love for baking. That's the CinaRoll promise.

Our Bits

Colorfully Frosted Rolls

Colorful Luxury for Your Celebrations! Our rich, classic cinnamon rolls are topped with our delicious signature cream cheese frosting and customizable colorful sprinkles and toppings. Perfect for parties, birthdays, or just adding joy to your day. Available in ready-made or custom assortments.

Classic Rolls

The Flavor That Started It All! Known for its superior flavor and far-reaching aroma, thanks to our authentic Makara® Cinnamon sourced from the mountains of West Sumatra. Combined with our smooth, tender dough and fluffy signature frosting, these are not your average cinnamon roll. Available in boxes of 4, 6, or 12 rolls.

BonBites

The Perfect Bite of Happiness! Enjoy the same classic, distinctive flavor in a perfectly sized, mini treat. BonBites are the quickest and sweetest way to experience the richness of Makara® Cinnamon and our indulgent cream cheese frosting. Ideal for sharing or a quick sweet fix. Family boxes contain 16 pieces.

Topped Bons

Limitless Customization! Elevate your cinnamon roll experience with our gourmet toppings. Indulge in our famous selections like the rich Caramel Pecanbon®, the decadent OREO®Bon, and more. Choose your preferred size: Classic Rolls or MiniBon® Rolls
